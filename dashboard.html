About the World

Berlin — The Fallen One is a dark fantasy web novel series set in the shattered realm of Varenhal, where the gods tore themselves apart and left their believers to survive in the silence.

The Chronicles of the Forgotten Knight follows a disgraced warrior who wakes in a mass grave with no memory and a sealed mark on his chest — the mark of something the gods wanted buried.

"He did not seek redemption. He sought the truth — and the truth was worse than damnation."
