The Chronicles of the
Forgotten Knight

By Berlin

Synopsis

In the shattered realm of Varenhal, gods once walked among men — until the Fracture tore heaven apart and left their servants to fend for themselves in a world gone cold.

A knight wakes in a mass grave. He has no name. No memory. Only a sealed mark on his chest that pulses like a second heartbeat — and a nagging feeling that he has died before.

As he scrapes together the pieces of who he was, he discovers that someone very powerful wanted him forgotten. And they are not done with him yet.
