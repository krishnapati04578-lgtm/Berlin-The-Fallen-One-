Lore & World

World Notes

The Realm of Varenhal

Varenhal is a continent scarred by the Fracture — an event centuries past when the gods went to war with each other and shattered heaven. The fallout remade the land: coastlines cracked, forests turned silver-black, and the sky at night shows not stars but the pale debris of divine battlefields.

The Fracture

No one living witnessed the Fracture. Historians piece it together from contradictory scripture and the ruins it left. What is agreed: the gods chose sides. Chose violence. And lost something essential in the process. Those who survived are diminished, absent, or mad.

The Seal

Certain individuals are born — or made — with Seals: marks that bind them to divine remnants. Seals are rarely visible, rarely stable, and dangerous to bear. They attract attention from the wrong kinds of power.

Key Locations

  • Vethkar — A city built on the ruins of a god's corpse. Its amber stone is warm to the touch. Its politics are cold.
  • The Pale Wall — A fortification of unknown age that separates the western territories from the wild lands beyond.
  • Ashenveil — A settlement on the edge of the Pale Wall. Militarized, haunted, and currently under siege.
